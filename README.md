## Who I Am

My name is Lawrence Eubanks III. I spent 27 years as a water and wastewater treatment operator on the coast of California. I retired in October 2024 and made a decision — if I have to earn an income, it has to be from home, doing something that keeps me learning.

That brought me to cybersecurity.

I am in year four of learning. No bootcamp background. No computer science degree. Just a man who decided late was better than never, and that confusion is part of the process, not a sign to stop.

This repo documents the journey.

## The Business

**BreadboardPro.com** — a cybersecurity education platform built for people who started late, feel behind, or cannot relate to the "I got hired in six months" success stories.

If you are over 40, changing careers, or just trying to understand what cybersecurity actually is — this is built for you.

The goal is not a job title. The goal is to become someone who understands how systems work, how they break, and how to protect them. That knowledge has value whether you are building a business, protecting your own network, or helping others do the same.
